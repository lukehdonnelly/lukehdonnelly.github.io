About

Every year, 800,000 people die to suicide, meaning that one person dies to suicide every 40 seconds. For every person who has died to suicide, more than twenty others have made an attempt at suicide. This means that one person attempts suicide about every 2 seconds or 16,000,000 attempt suicide in total every year.

Out of all the people who commit suicide worldwide, 6 out of eight of them are males. This is why we are raising awareness for mens mental health, to help reduce this number.

Two thirds of people with a mental health disorder do not seek help from a mental health professional help. Professional help is very important as it is very rare that people will get over their mental illnesses withought help.

OUR ISSUE

Our issue is mental health and wellbeing. Mental illness is a term which refers to a group of multiple different illness. A mental illness is a health problem what changes the way that someone thinks, feels, behaves and interacts with others. Mental health problems (which are like mental illness but are less bad) can be experienced by people as a reaction to certain stresses. Some of the major types of mental illnesses are anxiety, bipolar mood disorder, eating disorders, depression, schizophrenia and personality disorders. The most common are anxiety and depression where feelings of fear, tension and sadness become so intense that the person has difficulty coping with common activities. Times where people have mental health problems and mental illness can come and go throughout periods of someone’s life. Some people can experience on episode of illness and fully recover whereas it can recur for others. It is rarely possible for someone with mental illness to make it go away with strength of will. The risk of suicide is higher for people with mental illnesses especially after diagnosis.

MOVEMBER




Movember is a fundraising organisation that encourages the growing of moustaches in November to raise awareness for men’s mental health, suicide prevention, prostate, and testicular cancer. By 2030 they aim to reduce male suicide by 25%. Movember looks at mental health from the perspective of males. Their plan includes: Advocating for men by encouraging governments and law to understand the struggles that men deal with. Services by working to make services that are specific to males. Conversations by working toward a world that men feel comfortable having a conversation about big things like mental health. Education by helping men to build connections socially and take action early.

WHOS AFFECTED

Mental health is a problem which affects all men and women, but especially men. The families and friends of those who commit suicide are also affected. Mental health and well-being affect men in very serious ways. Mental illness impact people in that their quality of life is lower than most people and they struggle doing everyday activities. Mental illnesses can often result in a person becoming depressed or even committing suicide.

What to do if you are feeling depressed:


Heathy eating, regular physical activity and getting lots of sleep are important to good mental health. Also, learning skills that help you to deal with stress, relationships, feeling down or the symptoms of your mental illness are other ways that you can look after yourself. Simply talking to someone and venting can make you feel better.
